Welcome to Elijah's budget calculator!

This project was created with the express purpose of calculating monthly expenses, disposable income, and debt-to-income ratios and much more. By reporting these metrics as a percentage of income, the goal is to give a simple, quick and intuitive representation of expenses. Please see the usage section of this website for intructions on usage and documentation.
